    2. Prompt Categories: The categories reflect the knowledge pre requisites that are required by students to write the prompt.
      • Low: Assumption there is low to no knowledge available and there will be primarily a copy & paste job of the exercise description.
      • Medium: Assumption there is medium knowledge about the subject available to write the prompt. This could be:
        • GenAI Prompting Know-How: Telling the AI to act in a specific role (e.g. Project Manager) and only include a relevant subset of the provided exercise use-case.
        • Subject Knowhow: Providing used key terminologies from the lecture
      • High: Assumption there is strong knowledge about the subject available to write the prompt: This could be:
        • GenAI Prompting Know-How: Telling the AI to act in a specific role (e.g. Project Manager) and only include a relevant subset of the provided exercise use-case. Furthermore, specify more concretely what to expect in the response.
        • Subject Knowhow: Making a concrete example of a good response that should be replicated and adopted by the GenAI. Use specific terminologies from the lecture and provide hints to create a good response.
